About App

Know your Indian train berth/seat position of II-Tier AC, III-Tier AC, Garib Rath, Sleeper Class, Second Seating, Second Seating in JanShatabdi and for most of the Indian trains.

Its a complete off-line application, means you do not require, active Internet connection to use this application.

Simply download and install, that's what you need to do

Just enter your berth/seat number and check it's exact position.
